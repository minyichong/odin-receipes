Peanut Butter and Pumpkin Dog Treats

Description

If you want to get some attention from your dog or make them happy; you gotta make these dog treats. A healthy and easy snack for your hoomans.

Perfect during the fall harvest season !

Ingredients

  • 2 and 1/2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup canned/fresh pumpkin puree
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Whisk together the flour, eggs, pumpkin, peanut butter, salt, and cinnamon in a bowl. Add water as needed to help make the dough workable, but the dough should be dry and stiff.
  3. Roll the dough into a 1/2-inch-thick roll.
  4. Cut into 1/2-inch pieces and roll to shapes you desired.
  5. Bake in preheated oven until hard, about 40 minutes.
  6. Place these treats in a reseable bag or jar. You now have the perfect dog treat and expect it to be gone in a week !
